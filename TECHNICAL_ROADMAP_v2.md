# HandicapLab Technical Roadmap v2

This document charts the roadmap of the HandicapLab platform toward SaaS product release, focusing on model reliability and research precision.

---

## Roadmap Milestones

| Version | Milestone | Scope / Deliverables | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **v1.1** | **Live Ingestion Platform** | Adapters, Event Bus, Raw/Normalized/Curated storage, quality checks. | ✅ Complete (Sprint 26) |
| **v1.2** | **Historical Dataset & Replay** | EPL 10–15 seasons, Parquet datasets, leakage-proof historical replay. | 🔜 Sprint 27 |
| **v1.3** | **Feature Engineering v2** | ELO progression, rolling xG/xGA, squad, scheduling, cuaca, referee. | ⏳ Sprint 28 |
| **v1.4** | **Backtest & Bet Simulation** | ROI, Yield, CLV, Kelly drawdown, and portfolio simulation. | ⏳ Sprint 29 |
| **v1.5** | **Multi-League Expansion** | La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, Eredivisie, etc. | ⏳ Sprint 30 |
| **v2.0** | **Advanced Markets & release** | BTTS engine (Sprint 31-32), Stripe monetization, OAuth, dashboards. | ⏳ Planned |
