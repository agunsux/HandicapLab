# VALUE BETTING PHILOSOPHY MANUAL

**Platform Strategy:** Value Betting Intelligence Platform (EPIC 36)

---

## Strategic Positioning Matrix

| Platform | Core Question Answered | Primary Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **FootyStats** | *"Who is likely to win?"* | Match statistics & raw win probabilities |
| **RebelBetting** | *"What is the market price?"* | Price scanner & bookmaker arbitrage |
| **HandicapLab** | **"Where is the market wrong, why is EV positive, and what is the empirical evidence proof?"** | **Mathematical EV, Fair Odds, Historical Cohorts & Live Validation Audit** |

---

## Core Principles

1. **Negative EV Exclusion**: Recommendations with EV &lt; 0.0% are strictly classified as `NO_VALUE` or `PASS` and prohibited from action.
2. **Side-by-Side Fair Odds**: Model Fair Odds are always displayed alongside Bookmaker Market Odds to highlight mispricing magnitude.
3. **Closing Line Value (CLV)**: CLV retention is monitored as the primary surrogate for long-term expected returns.
